Early Life and Career

Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He started playing cricket at a young age and showed great potential as a batsman. He played for the Delhi Under-15 team and later, the Delhi Under-19 team. In 2006, he made his debut for the Indian national team in an ODI against Sri Lanka.

Over the years, Kohli has become one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He has scored more than 12,000 runs in ODIs and more than 7,000 runs in Tests. He has also captained the Indian national team in all formats of the game.

Philanthropy Work

  • Virat Kohli Foundation

    The Virat Kohli Foundation was founded in 2013 with a mission to empower underprivileged children and youth. The foundation focuses on education and sports, providing scholarships and training programs to help young people achieve their full potential.
